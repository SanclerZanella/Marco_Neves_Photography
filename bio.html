Family, Love and Photography

It's a huge pleasure to have you here. I'm Marco, 23 years old, and a lover of wedding photography and family photography. Since the first contact with photography, I knew it was exactly the path that I wanted to follow. I have a huge passion to know people and stories, and I wanted my photography to be based on that, tell stories. It's really good to sit with friends to see old photos that remember incredible moments, and special people. And it was that feeling that propelled me to be a professional photographer, thus being able to provide to people that love the life like me, eternalize the moments of his stories.

Photography is an instrument that I use to tell family stories. The union of a couple, the waiting and arrival of a new family member, and any other special family member that deserves to be registered. When photographing, I take the opportunity to connect with people, with stories. It's a moment of approach, it's more than being photographed and directed, it's also being heard and welcomed. I care about the stories, each one. They are the reason for my work, they give meaning to what I do.

"PHOTOGRAPHY IS FOR THE FUTURE"
(Fernanda Suhett)

this phrase changed my conception of photography and opened my eyes to new horizons, such as the photography of pregnant women. I realized that pictures of the belly, trousseau pieces, flapping dresses ... These are beautiful memories, however, the photograph of a pregnant woman must go much further, it is very interesting and satisfying when we expand the look to the family, because there is the story, the emotion.
