The Sweet Waiting for Benjamin

Those who see these photos cannot imagine the rain that came that day. On the way to the session Tatiane called me saying that it was raining a lot on the way going to the session site, I was already getting a little scared, because it was starting a huge storm. But then it was clearing the weather and we had a beautiful scenery with the clouds drawing the sky, and we still got a little bit of the sun in the late afternoon. And we were recording the week before little Ben arrived.
